DO'S

  • Buy only authorized crackers from licensed sellers.
  • Store crackers in a cool, dry place, away from fire sources.
  • Always light crackers in an open area, away from buildings.
  • Keep a bucket of water or sand nearby for emergencies.
  • Wear cotton clothes while bursting crackers.
  • Supervise children while they burst crackers.
  • Dispose of used crackers by soaking them in water.

DON'TS

  • Don't light crackers in your hand.
  • Don't burst crackers near flammable substances.
  • Don't wear synthetic clothes while bursting crackers.
  • Don't try to relight crackers that fail to ignite.
  • Don't burst crackers indoors or in confined spaces.
  • Don't allow children to burst crackers without supervision.
  • Don't throw or point crackers at others.
